Constantly improving your journey

When you review your route and report any incidences into the system, Amble acknowledges them and adjusts the system making you and your fellow Amblers feel increasingly safe.

We want women to enjoy the journey as much as the destination. Currently, maps are designed with cars in mind and at Amble we believe in people first. Whether you're walking or on public transport we help you get from A to B easily and safely.

What makes us unique?



Streaming sites know us well enough to recommend TV shows we might like, or music we want to listen to, yet it is absurd that maps don’t recognise the different ways we like to navigate the city. Other maps allow you to avoid a traffic jam, a toll way or provide you with rain safe routes so why can’t we avoid an unlit street, a park, or a hotspot for inappropriate behaviour? At Amble, we are putting you at the heart of your journey.

We want women to enjoy the journey as much as the destination. Currently, maps are designed with cars in mind and at Amble we believe in people first. Whether you're walking or on public transport we help you get from A to B easily and safely.
