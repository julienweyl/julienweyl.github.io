Change the way people move around cities

A navigation app giving women the freedom and confidence to move around the city with ease.


Features

Map

Amble is a personalised navigational app that integrates optimal walking routes with public transport data to help you move around the city. Want to get from a to b feeling safe and comfortable? Then this is the map for you.

Report

We have created a decentralised reporting system that enables you to review your route and report any issues with the cities infrastructure, incidence of harassment or any issue that has caused you discomfort.

Customized

Everyone is different and Amble recognises that. We allow you to set preferences in the app and ensure that your route accounts for the sorts of things that you want and don’t want in your journey.

Reinventing navigation... with you in mind!

In a world where streaming sites know us well enough to recommend TV shows we might like, or music we want to listen to, it seems absurd that maps don’t recognise the different ways we like to navigate the city. Other maps allow you to avoid a traffic jam or a toll way, so why can’t we avoid an unlit street, a park, or a hotspot for inappropriate behaviour? This is all about to change with Amble as we put you at the centre of your journey.

Core principle

Personal level

We want to empower women to make informed decisions to improve their mobility. They can review their route, report any issues or incidence creating a community of travellers invested in each other’s safety.

Structural level

A decentralised reporting system will capture data that will allow police, councils, and public transport systems to respond to the functional aspects of public spaces that impact safety.

Cultural level

We want to catalyse cultural change to transform public spaces in order to reduce incidence of harassment, improve the infrastructure, and create safer, and enjoyable spaces for all.
