What do we do?

Our vision is to change the way people feel and move around cities with our first mission focused on helping women to feel safer moving around public spaces.

Light Your Way will build a community of women who feel responsible and accountable for each other, enabling them to reclaim public spaces and helping them to pick their optimal route home based on global standards for safety, and their individual preferences.
