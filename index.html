What is amble?

Amble is a navigational app that provides personalised safety routes for women.

We want women to enjoy the journey as much as the destination. Currently, maps are designed with cars in mind and at Amble we believe in people first. Whether you're walking or on public transport we help you get from A to B easily and safely.





Personalisation

Every person is different and we give you the tools to design your route based on your own unique preferences.



Smart routing

Unlike other navigational apps we don't just optimise for time but route you down the streets you feel safest enabling you to avoid green spaces, unlit streets, quiet streets and alleyways.

Reporting

Amble allows you to review your route and report issues or incidences you encounter ensuring that your route and the of your fellow Amblers continuously improves.

Voice enabled

We know that voice is the future and how we as humans like to connect with the world around us. Instead of looking down at your phone, we allow you to enjoy your surroundings whilst we navigate you by point of interest

The problem
